How to make pancakes

  1. Pour 2/3 - 3/4 of a cup of water into a bowl.
  2. Pour 1 cup of pancake mix into the bowl.
  3. Stir until satisfied with the consistency.
    • For thicker pancakes, add more mix.
    • For thinner pancakes, add more water.
  4. Preheat griddle to 375-400 Fahrenheit.
  5. Spray nonstick spray onto the surface of the griddle.
  6. Pour portions of the mix onto the griddle so that they form circles.
  7. Wait a couple of minutes and then flip the pancakes when they appear how you like.
  8. Wait another minute or so and plate the pancakes.
  9. Drizzle syrup on them or use whatever toppings you like.
