How to make a pizza

  1. Pour 1 cup of rising flour into the bowl.
  2. Pour 3/4 cup of greek yogurt on top of the flour. Kneed together until they are mixed thoroughly into a dough-like texture.
  3. Roll the dough out using a pin roller so that it is circular with uniform thickness.
  4. Fold the edge inward slightly to make the crust.
  5. Pour several tablespoons of marinara sauce onto the dough. Use a spoon to spread across evenly.
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top and place whatever other toppings you like as well.
  7. Preheat oven to 400 Fahrenheit and bake for 20-25 minutes.
