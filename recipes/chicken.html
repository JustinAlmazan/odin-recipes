How to make chicken

  1. Marinate the chicken in your marinade of choice anywhere from 2-24 hours (I prefer Mojo Criollo).
  2. Get a baking pan and place a layer of tinfoil over it.
  3. Cut the chicken into smaller strands if it hasn't been already so that it resembles a tender.
  4. Pound the meat if you think it is too thick.
  5. Place the tenders on the pan.
  6. Pour some oil onto the pan and lightly move them around in the oil to get the surface covered.
  7. Pour spices on them. Some of my favorites include:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Paprika
    • Chili Powder
  8. Bake at 400 Fahrenheit until the inside temperature reaches 165 degrees (should take about 15 minutes).
